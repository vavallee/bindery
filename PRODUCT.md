# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Self-hosters and home-library maintainers use Bindery to acquire, organize,
and monitor ebook and audiobook collections. They need dependable workflows
that stay understandable while managing metadata, downloads, and automation.

## Product Purpose

Bindery is a resilient Readarr replacement that manages book discovery,
monitoring, downloading, and import without depending on one metadata source.
Success means users can maintain a library with little operational overhead and
can understand what the application will do before automation runs.

## Brand Personality

Clear, dependable, and quietly capable. The interface should feel direct and
trustworthy, with confident language and minimal ceremony around routine work.

## Anti-references

Avoid dated media-manager interfaces, decorative dashboard treatments, and
novel controls that obscure standard actions. Do not imitate the visual density
or brittle workflows associated with legacy Readarr-style administration.

## Design Principles

- Put the user's current task before configuration.
- Reveal automation choices when they become relevant.
- Make system state and consequences explicit.
- Reuse familiar controls and consistent interaction patterns.
- Keep common paths compact without hiding advanced control.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast and interaction requirements. Support keyboard and screen
reader navigation, visible focus, clear inline errors, reduced motion, responsive
layouts, and language-independent UI through the existing i18n system.
